Craft is meant to serve a purpose. Initially, women used to make a variety of utilitarian as well as ornamental products. Traditionally. they used to make different types of baskets like big containers with lids to store food. Flower baskets, beautiful small boxes with caps to keep jewellery, dry fruits and trays for fresh fruits, betel leaf and nuts. Today, artisans are making different types of decorative as well as utilitarian products as per the demand of the market. Coasters, hand-held fans, mirror frames, pencil boxes. 3D figures, mobile cases are made for the urban market.
